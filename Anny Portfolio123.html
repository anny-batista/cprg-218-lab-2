About Me

Hi, I'm Anny Batista, a second-year student of the NMPD program. My passion lies in creating visual art, with vectorization being my favorite method of expression. I enjoy exploring various artistic techniques and experimenting with different mediums to bring my ideas to life. Art has always been a central part of my life, fueling my creativity and allowing me to express myself in unique ways. Outside of my artistic pursuits, I find joy in immersing myself in nature, capturing its beauty through photography, and delving into the pages of captivating books. Each day brings new inspiration and opportunities for artistic exploration, and I'm excited to continue growing as an artist and discovering new avenues for creative expression.

Featured Works

Mindfulness

Immerse yourself in a serene sanctuary of mindfulness with this digital artwork created in Adobe Illustrator. At the center, the word "Mindfulness" stands boldly, surrounded by celestial objects such as the Sun, Moon, and Stars, symbolizing balance and the eternal nature of the universe. Interwoven with the cosmic elements are delicate mushrooms and winding vines, evoking a sense of interconnectedness with nature. Warm, earthy tones dominate the color palette, inviting viewers to connect with the earth and find solace in its nurturing embrace. Ultimately, this artwork serves as a poignant reminder to pause, breathe, and embrace the present moment, finding joy in the simple marvels of everyday life.

Solace

"Solace" is a digital artwork crafted in Adobe Illustrator, capturing the theme of finding refuge and resilience amidst life's struggles. Central to the composition is a cottage, symbolizing sanctuary amidst adversity, inspired by the biblical story of Mary and Joseph's journey to Bethlehem. Above the cottage shines a radiant Star, reminiscent of divine guidance, leading weary travelers to safety and peace. Within the cottage, a solitary flower blooms, representing the resilience of the human spirit in the face of hardship. Presented in a Tarot card format, "Solace" invites contemplation of destiny and possibility. Surrounding foliage symbolizes the omnipresence of God and the enduring beauty of creation. The color palette, featuring shades of purple and beige, adds to the artwork's mysticism and simplicity, evoking spirituality and warmth. In essence, "Solace" is a poignant reflection on finding peace amidst life's trials, inviting viewers to reflect on their own journeys and find solace in moments of grace.
